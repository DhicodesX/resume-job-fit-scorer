EXPECTED OUTPUTS FROM RESUME-TO-JOB FIT SCORER

1. Job Fit Score (0–100%):
- Final score representing how well the resume matches the job description.

2. Matched Skills:
- List of skills present in both the resume and the job description.

3. Missing Skills:
- List of required skills not found in the candidate's resume.

4. Experience Match:
- Comparison of required experience vs actual experience.
- Output: Fully Matched / Partially Matched / Not Matched.

5. Education Match:
- Comparison of required degree vs candidate's degree.
- Output: Matched / Partially Matched / Not Matched.

6. Keyword Match Score (0–100%):
- Percentage of job-related keywords present in the resume.

7. Recommendations:
- Suggestions to improve resume relevance.
- Examples:
  - Add missing technical skills.
  - Mention specific tools or technologies used.
  - Highlight experience with APIs, databases, frameworks, etc.
